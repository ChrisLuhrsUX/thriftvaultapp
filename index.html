Features

Inside the vault.

Unlimited AI scans, pricing & vault

Snap anything off the rack. In seconds, you'll see what it's worth, what you'd make, and how sure we are. No scan limits, no item limits.

Handmade & upcycle pricing

Crochet, hand-knit, upcycles, frankenpieces, priced against actual Depop sold listings, not retail tags or labor-hour math.

Counterfeit & scam alerts on every scan

Red flags call out likely fakes, stock-photo listings, and brand giveaways to verify before you buy: date codes, hardware stamps, stitch counts.

Flip ideas & before/after pricing

Three flip ideas per scan, each in a different aesthetic. Snap before-and-after photos and we'll price the finished piece, not the thrifted base. Wrong guess? Rescan.

Hauls, profits, and what's actually selling

Group each store run into a haul. See what you've spent, what you've made, and what's actually moved. No spreadsheets.

Yours, on your phone

No sign-up, no cloud, no account to forget. Everything you scan stays right on your phone.
